# Privacy Policy for Bullseye

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Bullseye ("the app") is a long-range shooting ballistics calculator developed by
Evermark Systems ("we," "us"). This policy explains what the app
accesses on your device and how that information is handled. In short: Bullseye works
entirely on your device. We do not operate a server, we do not have user accounts, and
we do not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal data.

## Information the app accesses

Bullseye does not require an account and does not ask you for personal information. To
provide its features, the app may use the following device capabilities, all processed
locally on your device:

- **Location (GPS).** Used only to compute the Coriolis effect and magnetic declination
  for a more accurate firing solution, and to record the conditions at the time of a
  logged shot. Your location is used on the device and is never sent to us or to any
  third party.
- **Bluetooth and nearby devices.** Used only to connect to optional environmental
  sensors (such as anemometers and weather meters) so the app can read live wind,
  temperature, pressure, and humidity. On some Android versions the operating system
  requires the location permission in order to scan for Bluetooth devices; the app does
  not use this permission to track you.

The app does not access your contacts, photos, microphone, or camera.

## Data stored on your device

Your profiles, shot logs, settings, and sensor readings are stored locally on your
device only. This information is not uploaded to us or to any external service.
Uninstalling the app removes this data.

## Sharing features

The app includes an optional feature that lets you export your own shot-string data to a
file and share it with people you choose, using apps already on your device (for example
a messaging app or Bluetooth). This sharing is initiated entirely by you and goes
directly to the recipient you select. We do not receive, store, or process these files.
By default, location information is removed from exported files; including it is an
explicit, optional choice you control.

## Data collection and third parties

We do not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share your personal data. The app contains no
advertising, no analytics or tracking SDKs, and no third-party data-collection services.

## Children

Bullseye is intended for an adult audience and is not directed to children.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an
updated "Last updated" date.
